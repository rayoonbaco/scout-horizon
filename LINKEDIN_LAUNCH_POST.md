# LinkedIn Launch Post

I built a project called **Operation: Retrieve Gomez**.

The joke is simple: I am not exactly an elite outdoorsman.

But the lesson behind the project is serious:

**Getting lost is expensive. Situational awareness matters.**

So I built **Strategic Radar — Scout Mode**, a demo-safe AI-assisted dashboard for life sciences leadership.

It turns fragmented public/sample signals into:

- market and competitor intelligence
- opportunity lanes
- risk flags
- confidence cues
- a plain-English Scout Brief
- next-action review

It is not enterprise production software. It is a proof-of-work project showing how messy signal overload can become a cleaner decision surface.

**I get lost. AI finds the way.**

Built by a scout. Powered by AI.

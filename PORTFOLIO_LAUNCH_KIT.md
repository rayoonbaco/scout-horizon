## One-sentence description
A demo-safe executive decision-intelligence dashboard that turns fragmented public/sample life-sciences automation signals into early warnings, opportunity lanes, confidence cues, and next-action review.

## What it demonstrates
- Modeling a leader's decision environment from sparse discovery.
- Turning messy signal overload into a curated executive review surface.
- Building a real FastAPI + browser dashboard with filters, signal details, scoring cues, and demo-safe data.
- Protecting privacy while still telling a credible proof-of-work story.
- Packaging a local prototype for hosted Render deployment.

## Public-safe story
This was built as a demo-safe AI-builder proof asset. It does not expose private names, employer references, customer information, credentials, or regulated operational data. The Scout Mode identity is public-safe design language: patient observation, terrain awareness, horizon scanning, and decisive action.

## What to avoid saying publicly
Do not claim this is enterprise production software, FDA-compliant, validated GxP software, used by a named company, built for a named person, or powered by proprietary/private customer data.

## Short launch post draft
I built Strategic Radar — Scout Mode as a demo-safe executive decision-intelligence dashboard. The goal was to show how an AI-assisted builder can take sparse real-world discovery and turn messy signal overload into a useful executive review surface.

It uses a FastAPI backend, a browser dashboard, curated public/sample life-sciences signals, scoring cues, filters, an active-signal detail pane, and a Scout Brief that summarizes what changed, why it matters, pressure level, and next move.

This is not enterprise production software or a regulated validated system. It is a hosted proof-of-work demo showing how I think, build, anonymize, package, test, and explain AI-assisted decision-support tools.

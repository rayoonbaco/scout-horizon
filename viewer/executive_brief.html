What Strategic Radar is

The short version

Strategic Radar is a dark, executive-facing scout console. It takes fragmented external signals and converts them into a cleaner decision surface: what changed, why it matters, how urgent it is, and what to review next.

  • Curated public/sample signal set
  • Signal lanes, scoring, and filters
  • Plain-English Scout Brief
  • Active Signal detail view
  • Hosted and demo-safe presentation layer

Why the scout metaphor works

The product is not trying to be a giant noisy feed. It is built to survey the field before the field changes — scan the horizon, notice movement early, and turn scattered signs into a better executive first look.

Why it is cool

It turns signal overload into a decision surface

This is more than search. The app reshapes information into an executive review surface: watchlist, brief, and next move.

It feels like a real product

Scout Mode gives the system a memorable identity. The app looks intentional, hosted, and demo-safe rather than like a rough prototype.

It is refresh-ready

The project supports a current-source refresh path, which gives it a near-real-time feel in demos without overclaiming enterprise streaming.

# Strategic Radar — Executive Summary

## What it is
Strategic Radar is a demo-safe executive scout console that turns fragmented public/sample life-sciences automation signals into a curated watchlist, a plain-English Scout Brief, and practical next-review guidance.

## Why it stands out
- **Distinct product identity:** The dashboard is not a generic feed. It presents itself as a scout console — built to survey the field before the field changes.
- **Current-source refresh path:** The system can ingest and refresh watched public/sample sources on demand, keeping the radar timely and useful for live demos.
- **Executive framing:** Instead of dumping raw links, it turns signals into priorities, confidence cues, lanes, and a next move.
- **AI-builder proof:** It shows the ability to take sparse stakeholder discovery and turn it into a useful decision-support surface.
- **Demo-safe:** The project avoids private company/customer/employee details and keeps the content public-safe.
- **Hosted and testable:** It has a clear local launch path, Render-ready deployment path, and internal smoke tests.

## What it proves
This project proves that messy external signal overload can be converted into a cleaner decision environment for a leader — without pretending to be enterprise production software.

## Best public one-liner

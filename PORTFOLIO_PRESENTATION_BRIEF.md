# Strategic Radar — Executive Presentation Talk Track

## Slide 1 — Executive summary
Strategic Radar is a demo-safe executive scout console. It turns fragmented public/sample signals into a concise watchlist, a Scout Brief, and practical next steps.

## Slide 2 — Why it is cool
This is impressive because it does not just collect information. It interprets what matters, organizes it into decision lanes, and gives a leader a cleaner first look.

## Slide 3 — What the product actually does
- Pulls or restores a curated signal set.
- Organizes signals into lanes.
- Scores signals for priority and confidence.
- Produces a plain-English briefing.
- Lets a viewer drill into the active signal.

## Slide 4 — Why it is a strong AI-builder proof asset
The system was built from sparse real-world discovery rather than a giant enterprise spec. That is a strong builder story: messy context in, useful product out.

## Slide 5 — Technical credibility
- FastAPI backend
- Viewer frontend
- JSON cache / state flow
- Local and Render launch path
- Internal smoke tests
- Demo-safe content discipline

## Slide 6 — Positioning
This is not pretending to be enterprise production software. It is a polished, hosted, executive-facing proof asset that demonstrates product thinking, AI-assisted building, and decision-environment modeling.
